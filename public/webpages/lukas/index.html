Course page for: Mobile Applications

About the course

This course aims to give the student knowledge about the Android platform, the tools for developing applications (apps) for this platform as well as how take advantage of the capabilities of Android devices, such as sensors and cameras.

Furthermore, we will take a look at designing and implementing user interfaces when working on a platform with vast difference between device screens.

Project

You should form groups of two to three people for the project. The project is open, and you are free to form the scope of the project, as long as your project complies with the minimum specifications.

The minimum requirements, i.e. the requirements to receive the minimum passed grade (02) are the following:

  • You must create an Android app.
  • The app must run on Android 4.0 and above.
  • The app must support both phones and tablets of different resolutions.
  • The app must use a range of advanced technology (minimum 2) in a meaningful way, these technologies could be:
    • Sensors.
    • Camera.
    • GPS.
    • Maps.
    • Services.

You will develop the project in groups, and we will have a round of presentations around the end of the course, where you will get to show-off what you have made.
